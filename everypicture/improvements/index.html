time is a funny thing...

I come here year after year, a little older - a little wiser

changing like the creek does

I sit in the places people I know sat so many years ago

wishing some were still here

wishing I could meet the family who built this place

but I am grateful for the memories made

and the bonds I will have for life

time passes, people change, yet this place remains the same

portal
